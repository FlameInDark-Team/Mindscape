## 🌱 1. MOOD GARDEN - Interactive Wellness Visualization

### What It Is
A beautiful, gamified garden that grows and evolves based on your mental health journey. Each plant represents an achievement and milestone in your wellness path.

### Features
- **10 Unique Plants** to unlock
- **Dynamic Garden States**: Empty → Wilting → Struggling → Budding → Growing → Thriving
- **Visual Progress**: Garden health reflects your check-in consistency and mood scores
- **Interactive**: Click plants to see details and unlock requirements
- **Animated**: Plants appear with spring animations and hover effects

### Plant Collection
1. **🌱 First Seed** - Complete your first check-in
2. **🌿 Growing Sprout** - 3 check-ins completed
3. **🌸 Blooming Flower** - 3-day streak achieved
4. **🌻 Radiant Sunflower** - Positive well-being (7+ avg, 5+ check-ins)
5. **🌳 Wisdom Tree** - 10 check-ins milestone
6. **🌸 Cherry Blossom** - 7-day streak master
7. **🌵 Resilient Cactus** - Perseverance through tough times
8. **🌹 Beautiful Rose** - Thriving well-being (8+ avg, 5+ streak)
9. **🪷 Peaceful Lotus** - Mindfulness champion (15+ check-ins)
10. **🌈 Rainbow Garden** - Ultimate wellness achievement (30+ check-ins, 7+ avg)

### Why It's Cool
- Makes mental health tracking fun and visual
- Provides tangible goals to work toward
- Celebrates both consistency and improvement
- Reduces stigma through playful design
- Encourages daily engagement
